# FlexCeiling Pro — Walkthrough Video Script

A ready-to-record script for a short explainer video (~8–10 minutes). Record your screen
with a free tool like **Loom**, **ScreenPal**, or **Clipchamp**, read the narration, and
do the actions in order. Each scene lists **what to show**, **where to click**, and a
**narration** line you can read out (or paraphrase).

> Tip: log in *before* recording so no password is shown on screen. Have one practice
> client and one draft quote ready so the demo flows smoothly.

---

### Scene 1 — Intro (≈30s)
- **Show:** the Dashboard.
- **Say:** "Welcome to the FlexCeiling Pro Quote and Invoice system. In this short video
  I'll show you how to create a quotation, turn it into a pro forma and a tax invoice, and
  send it to a client — everything you need day to day."

### Scene 2 — The layout (≈30s)
- **Show:** point your mouse along the **sidebar** on the left.
- **Click:** Dashboard, then Quotes, Pro Forma, Invoices, Clients — briefly.
- **Say:** "On the left is the menu: your Dashboard, then Quotes, Pro Forma, Invoices, and
  Clients. On a phone you'd tap the menu icon at the top to open this. Your name and a Sign
  out button are at the bottom."

### Scene 3 — The Dashboard (≈45s)
- **Show:** hover over the four cards at the top, then the charts.
- **Say:** "The Dashboard gives you the headline numbers — what's outstanding, what you've
  invoiced this month and overall, and your quote conversion rate. Below that: invoicing
  over the last six months, your quote pipeline, top clients, and your most recent
  documents. Click any recent row to open it."

### Scene 4 — Create a quotation (≈2m)
- **Click:** **Quotes** → **+ New Quotation**.
- **Do:** pick or type a **client**.
- **Say:** "Let's make a quotation. I'll choose an existing client — or I can just type a
  new client's details here and the system saves them automatically."
- **Do:** add a **reference/scope**, then fill the first **line item** — description, area,
  unit, rate.
- **Say:** "I'll describe the work, then enter the area and the rate — notice the amount
  calculates itself. I'll add another row with the plus button."
- **Do:** show the **VAT** and **Grand Total** updating; type a **Note**.
- **Say:** "VAT and the grand total update live at the bottom. Anything I type in the Note
  prints in red on the document. When I'm happy, I click Save — and there's the live total
  and save button pinned at the bottom."
- **Click:** **Save quotation**.

### Scene 5 — The document page (≈45s)
- **Show:** the saved quote page with the **PDF preview** on the right.
- **Say:** "Here's the saved quotation, with a live preview of the finished PDF on the
  right. Up top I can change the status — Draft, Sent, Won or Lost — edit it, print it, or
  share it."
- **Click:** **Open / Print PDF** → show the branded PDF → close the tab.
- **Say:** "Open / Print PDF gives me the clean branded document, ready to print or save."

### Scene 6 — Generate a Pro Forma (≈1m15s)
- **Click:** **Generate Pro Forma** on the quote.
- **Say:** "To request an advance payment, I click Generate Pro Forma. It copies everything
  and pre-fills a fifty-percent advance."
- **Do:** in the totals box, click the **50%** quick button; point at **Balance Due**.
- **Say:** "I can set the advance with these quick buttons or type an exact amount. The
  Balance Due — what's left to pay — updates automatically."
- **Click:** **Save changes** → **Open / Print PDF**.
- **Say:** "And the pro forma PDF shows the advance payment and the balance due in red."

### Scene 7 — Generate a Tax Invoice (≈45s)
- **Click:** open the quotation again → **Generate Tax Invoice**.
- **Say:** "When the client approves, I open the quote and click Generate Tax Invoice. It
  creates the official VAT invoice with a new number and the amount in words, and marks the
  quote as Won."
- **Show:** the invoice PDF preview (TRNs, totals, bank details, stamp).

### Scene 8 — Share with a client (≈45s)
- **Click:** **Share** on any document.
- **Say:** "To send it to a client, I click Share. This creates a private link that shows
  only this one document — the client doesn't need a login and can't see anything else."
- **Do:** show **Copy**, **WhatsApp**, **Email** buttons, then **Stop sharing**.
- **Say:** "I can copy it, or send it straight to WhatsApp or email. And I can stop sharing
  any time to switch the link off."

### Scene 9 — Find & export (≈45s)
- **Click:** **Quotes** → type in the **search box** → open **Filters**.
- **Say:** "To find anything, just type a client or number in the search. Filters let me
  narrow by status, client, date or amount."
- **Click:** **Export**.
- **Say:** "And Export downloads the list as a spreadsheet for the accountant."

### Scene 10 — Clients (≈30s)
- **Click:** **Clients** → open one client.
- **Say:** "The Clients section keeps all your customers in one place — open any client to
  edit their details and see every document you've made for them."

### Scene 11 — Wrap up (≈20s)
- **Show:** the Dashboard.
- **Say:** "That's the whole flow: quote, pro forma, tax invoice, and share — all in a few
  clicks. Keep your statuses up to date and the Dashboard stays accurate. Thanks for
  watching!"

---

**Total ≈ 9 minutes.** Keep each click slow and deliberate so viewers can follow. You can
trim any scene that isn't relevant to your team.
